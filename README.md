# Bookedup Marketing & Distribution

This repo tracks our marketing strategy, research, and execution plan for Bookedup.

## Quick Stats

- **Product:** Bookedup — lead capture platform for tradesmen
- **Target Audience:** Small contractors, plumbers, electricians, HVAC, roofers
- **Status:** Pre-customers, seeking first 10 users

## Marketing Strategy

1. **Facebook Groups** — local/regional contractor communities
2. **Supply Houses** — partnerships with material distributors
3. **Industry Associations** — NARI, local builder associations
4. **Direct Sales** — in-person demos at job sites
5. **Referral Partnerships** — piggyback on accountants, insurance agents
